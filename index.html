About Us

RKDN Digital LLC specializes in offering “CIO-as-a-service” expertise for non-profits, NGOs, public benefit corporations, and other enterprises with a mission to improve lives and benefit mankind. We strive to be a true partner with our clients, offering pragmatic advice on ways to adopt and adapt technologies to further their goals. We have extensive experience in evaluating off-the-shelf and custom-built digital solutions for their ability to meet a client’s needs.

Services

Legacy IT Modernization
Few organizations consider managing IT infrastructure their core competency, which means that it often gets little attention until something critical happens. We can assess ways to improve the availability, reliability, and security of your essential systems, while lowering the overall Total Cost of Ownership (TCO). We'll develop a roadmap and implementation plan to help get you where you want to be.
Application Development
If you think that a mobile or web application could radically transform how you collect data, interact with the community you serve, or just improve your overall operations, we can work with you to design, develop, deliver, and operate your applications.
Data Strategy
The data that you gather, create, and manage for the communities you serve could be an untapped asset, both within your organization and outside of it. We work to identify and assess the value of your data holdings, identifying when a shared, open data strategy might help accelerate your initiatives.
